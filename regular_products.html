Products

Below are a list of products that can be used to help combat the symptoms of SAD.

Image Link Description
Light Boxes Light boxes are used to mimic outdoor light. One of the main causes of SAD is the lack of sunlight so light boxes help to simulate natural light.
Vitamin D Pills The lack of sunlight can also lead to a Vitamin D deficiency, so Vitamin D supplements can offer a substitute.
Headspace Meditation App Meditation can help lead to a healthier, happier, and well-rested life through just a few minutes a day. Headspace has free options or a student discount available if you would like to purchase the premium package.
CAPS Northwestern Talking to a licensed professional can be the best way to combat feelings of depression.
