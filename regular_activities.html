Art Institute of Chicago


Free with Wildcard
Open everyday 10:30-17:00
111 S Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL

On Michigan Avenue, in the heart of Chicago’s historic downtown, between the iconic lions—frankly named "stands in an attitude of defiance" and "on the prowl"—lies nearly a million square feet and approximately 330,000 pieces of art to explore. These works span a wide stretch of time, ranging from antiquity to modernity.

Garfield Park Conservatory


Free (donation recommended)
Open everyday 9:00-17:00 (Wed 9:00-20:00)
300 N Central Park Ave, Chicago, IL

Often referred to as "landscape art under glass," The Garfield Park Conservatory is one of the largest and most stunning conservatories in the nation, where thousands of plant species are on display throughout eight rooms in this magnificent facility. Traveling through the conservatory allows visitors to experience lush flora and tropical temperatures away from the hustle and bustle of Chicago. Don’t forget to visit the 12 acres of stunning outdoor gardens.

$32.95

Lock Chicago provides unique Escape Rooms and Virtual Reality experiences perfect for team building, family, and friends of all ages! There are 3 Escape Rooms and a Virtual Reality Lounge which can be reserved for your group privately or publicly. Unlike other escape rooms teams here aren't physically locked in a room, rather they are working together to complete a mission before time expires.
